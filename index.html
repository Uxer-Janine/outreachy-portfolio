Data Scientist & Product Designer based in Kenya. Currently contributing to the Fedora ecosystem through Outreachy.

The Vision

My journey into tech began in design, where I learned how to approach complex problems creatively and keep users at the center of every decision. I now bring that same mindset into data science—asking the right questions, uncovering patterns, and translating insights into meaningful impact.

I recently completed an intensive Data Science bootcamp, building hands-on experience in Python, SQL, and core machine learning. My work spans exploratory data analysis, predictive modeling, and data visualization.

Outreachy Fedora Contribution

For my Outreachy application, I contributed to the Fedora ecosystem by working on Docling, focusing on multilingual document processing, and by improving RamaLama documentation. I designed and evaluated OCR pipelines, compared different approaches, and explored how structured outputs enable downstream AI applications.

I also contributed to the community by coordinating peer reviews, improving shared documentation, and engaging with contributors and mentors.

This portfolio brings together my technical work, writing, collaboration, and reflections—highlighting not only what I built, but also how I approach problem-solving, teamwork, and growth in open-source environments.
