Pipeline Overview

The goal was to convert unstructured documents (PDFs in Swahili and French) into structured Markdown format using a composite pipeline of Docling, Tesseract, EasyOCR, and Rapid OCR. The pipeline was developed and executed across both Windows and Fedora environments to ensure cross-platform reliability.
